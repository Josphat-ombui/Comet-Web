Synopsis

Comet Healthcares Limited

The brain child of Dr. Peter Asingo, a talented pharmacist in Kenya has quickly realised the fact that almost all medicine was being imported from abroad. Good medicine was expensive and out of the reach of the majority who were low and middle income earners. This led to the formation of Comet Healthcares Limited and a dream to manufacture quality and affordable medicines locally. Not only was that dream realised but Comet has grown to become the most reputable pharmaceutical manufacturer in east and central Africa.
